### Operating Cycle

1. **Vacuum Phase**: Motor-driven crankshaft pulls air from the outer chamber of the receiver
2. **Liner Contraction**: Silicone sleeve collapses inward, gripping the penis
3. **Pressure Phase**: Crankshaft pushes air back into outer chamber
4. **Liner Expansion**: Sleeve releases, creating upward/outward motion
5. **One-Way Valve**: Cap at receiver end maintains baseline suction level
6. **Repeat**: Continuous oscillation at user-controlled frequency (up to 180 SPM)

## Maintenance & Safety

### Hygiene

- Clean silicone sleeves after each use
- Replace liner when showing wear (cracks near connection points)
- Use water-based lubricant liberally

### Safety Features

- Overload cut-out in motor controller
- Air release valve for emergency pressure relief
- Viewing window for monitoring
- Red LED indicates machine cut-out

### Battery Note

Store remote upside down in slot to prevent accidental button presses and battery drain.
