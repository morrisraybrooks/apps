## How Air-Pulse Technology Works

### Basic Mechanism

1. **Sealed Chamber**: A soft silicone nozzle/tip creates an airtight seal around the clitoris
2. **Pressure Oscillation**: An internal actuator rapidly alternates between:
   - **Vacuum phase**: Creates negative pressure (suction)
   - **Release phase**: Returns to ambient or slight positive pressure
3. **Frequency**: These oscillations occur at frequencies typically between **1-20 Hz**
4. **No Direct Contact**: The pressure waves stimulate without touching the clitoris directly

### Patent Information (US10857063B2 - Novoluto GmbH)

Key technical elements from the Womanizer patent:
- **Valveless design**: Uses a flexible membrane/wall actuator instead of valves
- **Chamber construction**: Housing with stimulation chamber and flexible wall portion
- **Actuator types**: Electromagnetic, piezoelectric, or motorized membrane displacement
- **Pressure wave generation**: Reciprocating membrane creates alternating pressure
